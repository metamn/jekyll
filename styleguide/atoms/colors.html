Usage

Colors do not exists alone. They are forming color pairs: a foreground color on a background color.

Colors pairs have contrast. Better the contrast better the readability and perception.

Web standards suggest high enough color contrast ratios to make websites readable for people with color vision deficiency.

Color pairs

This site uses color pairs accessible for everybody. With a {% include site/atoms/link.html url="http://leaverou.github.io/contrast-ratio/" title="contrast ratio checker" %} we make sure all pairings pass the AAA or AA levels for any text.
